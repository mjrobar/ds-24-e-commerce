Asus NUC 14 Essential Core 3 N355 Mini PC

The Asus NUC 14 Essential Core 3 N355 Mini PC is a compact and versatile computing solution designed for everyday tasks, home entertainment, and business applications. Powered by the Intel Core 3 N355 processor, it delivers efficient performance while maintaining a small footprint, making it ideal for environments where space is at a premium. With support for high-speed DDR5 memory and NVMe storage, this mini PC ensures responsive multitasking and quick data access.

Efficient Performance with Low Power Consumption

Equipped with the Intel Core 3 N355 processor, the NUC 14 Essential offers a balance of power and efficiency. With a 15W TDP, it provides reliable performance for web browsing, document editing, and media streaming without excessive energy consumption. The integrated Intel Graphics further enhance the visual experience, supporting clear and vibrant displays for both work and play.

Multitask with Triple Display Support

Despite its small size, the Asus NUC 14 Essential is a powerhouse for productivity. It features multiple display outputs, including HDMI 2.1, DisplayPort 1.4, and USB-C with DisplayPort support, allowing you to connect up to three displays simultaneously. This triple display capability enables seamless multitasking, perfect for professionals who need an expanded workspace or home users looking for an immersive multi-monitor setup.
